Goa

7th Feb 2018 - 7th March 2018

An erstwhile Portuguese colony, Goa is a great example of how diverse India is. With a coastline of over 100 kilometers and hills nested in a small area, Goa has a lot to offer from pristine beaches, waterfalls, national parks, clubs, parties temples, churches, and much more.

For the foodies, Goan food is a treat with a fine blend of Portuguese and Indian flavors. That’s not all, being a traveler friendly destination, you name it and you’ll have it (almost)! For those looking for some spirit, Goa is the cheapest place to have alcohol in India ;) Isn’t that good enough already?

Like our other retreat, we will cover accomodation, breakfast, lunch and tons of fun activities!
