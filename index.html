Experience India with a community of Entrepreprenuers & Digital Nomads

Explore  .  Learn  .  Get Things Done

Remote Explorers is a retreat for location independent professionals (entrepreneurs, freelancers, remote workers, and explorers) who want to experience India while getting things done. Join us for a month in the Indian Himalayas with a community of like-minded folks. We are here to take care of everything from the internet to activities to make sure you can focus on what matters to you while having an experience of lifetime. Come, explore, learn, and get things done!

Mcleodganj, India (Home to H. H. Dalai Lama)

September 15 - October 15, 2017

Be free, like a bird! On a weekend during the retreat, we will arrange paragliding for all the participants in Bir-Billing, the venue for Paragliding World Cup 2016.
