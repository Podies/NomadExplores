Experience India with a community of Entrepreprenuers & Digital Nomads

Explore  .  Learn  .  Get Things Done

RemoteExplorers is a month long retreat for location independent people- entrepreneurs, freelancers, digital nomads- who want to get their work done, connect with like minded people, share experiences, and have fun in the Indian Himalayas. We take care of everything (stay, meals, activities, co-working space) so you can focus on what’s important to you!

Mcleodganj, India (Home to H. H. Dalai Lama)

September 15 - October 15, 2017

Activities

Mastermind Session

Set goals, get help, be accountable and accelerate your work.

Skill Share

Daily Standups

Share smiles every morning, set goals and get to a rocking start for the day.

Connect with Digital nomad community in india

Get to know and connect with digital nomad community in India.

Be where the action is - Experience new India

Get a first hand experience of a booming economing that is characterized with - chaos!

Paragliding in Himalayas

Nothing beats the sight of mighty Himalayas from the seat of a paraglider! Everyone gets a chance to do paragliding.

Mountain Survival Camp

Hike up a lonely mountain, collect, cook food and live like a primitive for a night in an old hut without electricity, phone signal!

Trekking in Himalayas

On one of weekends, we will trek the famous Triund and snowline trek(height of 3000 meters). Bonfires, camping and fun!

Yoga and Meditation

Yoga and meditation session led by an experienced Indian yoga teacher every morning on weekdays.

Experience local culture

One day tour to a village nearby. You get to meet local people, enjoy folk dance and music, and experience local food and life.

A Chance to listen to Dalai Lama

His Holiness Dalai Lama gives his annual preaching at about the same time as retreat and the venue of retreat is hardly 700 meters from his place, so you stand a chance to listen to Dalai Lama in person.

Learn about Buddhism and chat with monks

McloedGanj is a hub for tibbetan buddhists. You can watch and learn about buddhism from the buddhist monks.
