Remote Explorers is a retreat for location independent professionals (entrepreneurs, freelancers, remote workers, and explorers) who want to experience India while getting things done.

After our first successful digital nomad and entrepreneurs’ retreat in the Himalayas with 5 Star reviews, happy faces, and boost in participants’ productivity, it’s time to hit the beach!

Come, join us for a month in Goa full of experiences, fun, productivity, and lots of coconut water. Not to forget, we have choosen apartments with an access to a pool and a gym! As always, we take care of almost everything to help you focus on work and fun. We carefully curate the participants, because, community matters! Come, explore, learn, and get things done!
