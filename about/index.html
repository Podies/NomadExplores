About US

We are some of the early digital nomads in India connected by a common mission of driving the future of work in India. Apart from conducting retreats to showcase India and it’s potential to be the next digital nomad hub, we are also trying to inspire our fellow Indians through seminars, talks, and media. We are also helping companies to make a transition to remote work by providing training and consulting.
